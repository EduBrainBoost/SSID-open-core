# SSID Enterprise Adoption Disclaimer

## Purpose

This document provides mandatory disclaimers for enterprise adopters of the SSID protocol
and associated token systems.

## General Disclaimer

The SSID protocol and its associated tokens (SSID, vSSID, rSSID) are provided "as is"
without warranty of any kind, express or implied. Enterprise adopters are responsible
for their own due diligence, legal compliance, and risk assessment.

## Token Classification

SSID tokens are classified as **utility tokens** and **governance tokens** only.
They are not securities, financial instruments, investment contracts, or stores of value.
No representation is made regarding future token value or appreciation.

## Regulatory Compliance

Enterprise adopters must independently verify compliance with:
- Local securities regulations
- Anti-money laundering (AML) requirements
- Know-your-customer (KYC) obligations
- Data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- EU AI Act requirements (where applicable)

The SSID protocol provides privacy-preserving identity verification tools but does not
constitute legal advice or regulatory compliance certification.

## Non-Custodial Architecture

SSID operates on a strictly non-custodial basis:
- No centralized entity holds, manages, or controls user assets
- Private keys remain exclusively under user control
- No recovery mechanism exists for lost private keys
- No reversal mechanism exists for confirmed on-chain transactions

## Data Privacy

- SSID stores only SHA3-256 hashes of identity attributes, never raw PII
- Enterprise integrators must not circumvent this privacy model
- Data residency requirements are the responsibility of the integrating organization
- Federated learning models do not transmit raw user data
